I created this flyer when I was working with my friend on getting started with his musical performance class around UWB hub. We worked together, sat down, and decided who was going to do the promotion and who will put together the music albu, (list of songs to be played)

The "Resilient Visions 2020 UWB" program flyer was one of the few flyers that was created and presented as a prototype to the program organizer and our instructor professor. Minda J. Martin. I had included details such as the logo of the festival at the top, and text telling people where they can submit there work to be showcased during the festival. And included the organization that is sponsoring the event, AATV.

This was a color painting that I created using a fresh with coloring board and the colors that are sketched on this piece are black, red, orange, and yellow. It was seemingly easy combing the colors together as no difficulty had seem to risen. I created this because I absolutely love the radiant colors mixing together. And having to dive into loving and appreciating the narutal colors of nature.
